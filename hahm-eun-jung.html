    Hahm Eun-jung Facts

  • While growing up, she used to take ballet lessons.
  • Her mother started managing her career while Hahm Eun-jung was in 7th grade and for that, she had to quit teaching in 2007.
  • She made her debut as a member of T-ara in July 2009.
  • As a member of T-ara, Hahm Eun-jung released her debut studio album, Absolute First Album, on November 27, 2009, through Core Contents Media. The album comprised of songs such as Bo Peep Bo Peep, Like the First Time, You You You, Tic Tic Toc, Bye Bye, Wanna Play?, and Good Person and it was ranked at number #2 in Gaon Album Chart.
  • She has served as the main vocalist and lead rapper for T-ara.
  • In 2010, she played the role of Kang Seung-yeon in the television series, Coffee House, in which she starred alongside the likes of Kang Ji-hwan, Park Si-yeon, and Jung Woong-in. Hahm Eun-jung won the SBS Drama Award in the “New Star Award” category for her performance on the show.
  • Between 2011 and 2012, she appeared as one of the main cast members in MBC reality-variety show, We Got Married. For this, she won the MBC Entertainment Award in the “Best Newcomer (Variety)” in 2011.
  • As a solo artist, she released her debut EP, I’m Good, on May 7, 2015 by MBK Entertainment.
  • She has been trained in Taekwondo and has won a couple of competitions as well.
  • Regarding her mother, Hahm Eun-jung has said, “My mother had me 10 years after she was married to my father, which seems more hurtful. Upon watching several famous performers in the past, she wanted to become an actress. Now that I have, she is very pleased I have acting as a priority. She believes her original prayers were answered.”
