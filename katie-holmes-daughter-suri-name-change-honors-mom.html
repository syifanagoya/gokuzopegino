    When Katie Holmes’ daughter Suri reportedly dropped her dad Tom Cruise’s last name, she didn’t just pick a random name to go by instead. Apparently, the name honors her mom — and that makes the move even more special!

    A source told Page Six that the 18-year-old is now publicly going by Suri Noelle to “praise her mother.” “She is showing praise for her mother by using her middle name,” the source said, explaining that Holmes’ middle name is Noelle. The insider speculated that the name change came about because Suri “wants her own identity.” It will also help her “avoid the paparazzi” and allow her to “start fresh at college” this fall.     Now that Suri is 18, she could decide to publicly open up about her decision to change her name or talk about other controversial things in her family’s history.
