    Career

    Johnson started his journey with his YouTube channel ‘Ray William Johnson’ during his college time. His first videos were normal blogs that got a following of around 25-30 people. He became popular through his YouTube show ‘Equals 3’, which started in the middle of 2009, where he provided some humorous comments on viral videos. Due to this show, he became the first YouTube millionaire to acquire a net worth of $1 million.

    The show provided his channel 10 million subscribers and 2.6 billion total views at the end of the last episode by him, which broadcasted in March 2014 as he stated that he was unable to fulfill himself creatively anymore. The show returned in July 2014 with different hosts. Now the show is currently being hosted by Carlos Santos.

    His first scripted web series ‘Riley Rewind’ with Anna Akana, released at the end of 2013, consisting of 5 episodes, which got over 10 million views on Facebook, receiving a huge amount of appreciation from users as well as other magazines. During this series, he and Akana worked on a podcast, namely “Runaway Thoughts,” which interacted with the audience and discussed on general topics.

    Comedians On, Booze Lightyear, and Top 6 are some of his other great works, where the first two were canceled due to unavailability of Carlos Santos, as he was busy hosting the ‘Equals 3’ show. His studio produces these shows, “Equals Three Studios.”

    He is the executive producers of the ‘Top 6’ show, which is a “list show” written and hosted by none other than Kelly Landry, which talks about six items on a given topic.

    While living in New York (2010 to 2011), he started a blog viz ‘Breaking NYC,’ which covered his experiences in New York.

    Johnson has also tried his luck in a couple of movies like ‘The Fluffy Movie’ released in 2014 and ‘Who’s Driving Doug’ that was released in 2016, which received a rating of 5.3 and 3.9 from Rotten Tomatoes respectively.
