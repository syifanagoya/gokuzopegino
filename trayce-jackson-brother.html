    Trayce Jackson Brother: Who Is Tayven Jackson?: Born on 22 February 2000, Trayce Jackson, officially known as Trayce Jackson-Davis is an American college basketball player.

    He developed a passion for playing basketball at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after college basketball players.

    At the time of this writing (Friday, June 23, 2023), Jackson is a college basketball player for the Indiana Hoosiers of the Big Ten Conference.

    He attended Center Grove High School and as a junior, in 2017–18, he averaged 21.9 points, 9.4 rebounds, 4.1 blocks, and 2.6 assists per game while shooting 67.0 percent from the field.

    During his high school career, he was recruited by numerous high-profile schools, including Indiana, Michigan State, UCLA, Ohio State, Purdue, and Xavier, among others.

    On November 30, 2018, he committed to playing college basketball for Indiana after narrowing his choices down to Indiana, Michigan State, and UCLA.

    Internationally, he played for the United States under-18 basketball team at the 2018 FIBA Under-18 Americas Championship.

    On March 15, 2019, he was honored as the Indiana Gatorade Player of the Year for his athletic abilities and also for his academic discipline.

    In June 2023, Trayce Jackson hit the headlines following reports that he’s set to join the Golden State Warriors

    According to several online reports, the Warriors traded 2022 first-round pick. Patrick Baldwin Jr. to the Washington Wizards for the draft rights to Trayce Jackson.

    The reports also state that the deal will work into the Chris Paul blockbuster the teams agreed to earlier in the day (Friday, June 23, 2023).

    Trayce Jackson Brother: Who Is Tayven Jackson?

    Trayce Jackson is not the only child of his parents; Dale Davis (father) and Karla Jackson (mother).

    He has two sisters; Caida Davis, and Arielle Jackson, as well as a younger brother, Tayven Jackson.

    Tayven was a high school teammate at Center Grove and went on to play quarterback in college for the University of Tennessee for one year before transferring to Indiana University, where he has four years of eligibility.

    Trayce Jackson sisters live their lives away from the limelight hence their date of birth, age, educational background, and occupation was unknown at the time of this writing.
